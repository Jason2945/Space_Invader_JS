Greetings Captain!

Asteroids are falling onto Earth!
You are our last hope!

Instructions
1. Use the ← or → arrows to move the spaceship left and right.
2. Use the ↑ or ↓ arrows to move the spaceship up an down.
3. Use the space key to shoot out missles.
4. Watch out! If 3 asteroids pass you or hits you, you lose!
5. You only have 1000 missles. Use them wisely!
6. Every 50 asteroids destroyed will increase the speed of the following asteroids. Be careful!
